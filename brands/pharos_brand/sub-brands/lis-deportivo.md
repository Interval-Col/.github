## 2. Positioning

The LIS for **Biuman**'s sports laboratory — a **separate holding member** with a different
domain than clinical hematology (sports/performance testing vs clinical; RFC 0004 §3 runs LIS as
a separate instance per company). It carries its **own identity** (Deportivo, azul profundo)
while sharing the Pháros foundation — siblings by construction, not strangers.

> ⏸ **Open (RFC 0008 Q1/Q2):** whether this app sits fully under the Pháros umbrella
> (`Pháros · Deportivo`) or also carries Biuman's own brand is a naming call to confirm with
> @SKuger01 + the Biuman side.

descriptiva en mono. El acento de sub-marca **no** aparece como sub-nombre junto al logo.
